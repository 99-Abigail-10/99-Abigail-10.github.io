All About Abigail Adams

By Abigail Ashlit "Feilin" Kenyon

Abigail Adams was a woman who lived in the Revolutionary War. She had a husband named John Adams who fought in the war and she helped him out in the war. She also helped wounded soldiers. Abigail and her husband discussed the issues being debated by the Continental Congress.
